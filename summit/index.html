Call for proposals

How it works

  1. You submit a session (or sessions!) you'd like to present at the 2016 Red Hat Summit by January 11, 2016.
  2. Red Hat subject matter experts review each submitted abstract and give a numerical vote (1-5).
  3. Selection committees - made up of representatives across Red Hat - review the highest-rated sessions and build the agenda.
  4. You receive an accept or decline notification for each session you submitted.
