Certification news

Concentrate on the Red Hat Certified Architect skills you need

A Red Hat® Certified Architect (RHCA®) is a Red Hat Certified Engineer (RHCE®) or Red Hat Certified JBoss Developer (RHCJD) who has additionally attained Red Hat's highest level of certification. Earning RHCA is flexible. You can choose the set of eligible certifications beyond RHCE or RHCJD that best suit your professional needs, or you can follow one of our recommended concentrations below.

Datacenter
Prove your skills with tasks common in an on-premise datacenter.
Cloud
Demonstrate your skills with tasks common to cloud infrastructure.
Application platform
Focus your skills on tasks common for building and managing tools and applications.
Application development
Demonstrate knowledge of enterprise application development, integration, and architecture.
