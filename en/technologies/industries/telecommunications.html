Secure, open IT infrastructure

Whether shifting business models to capture new revenue, meeting explosive growth and unpredictable peaks in bandwidth demands, or fighting intense competition, communications companies like yours are facing significant change and growth.

Red Hat offers telecoms secure, open IT infrastructure. It can help providers:

  • Gain cost and operational efficiency.
  • Reduce complexity.
  • Accelerate time to market.
  • Maintain flexibility and growth, even within private, hybrid, and public cloud environments.

Network functions virtualization (NFV)

Open source to the core, the Red Hat NFV platform delivers the only complete software suite available to successfully bring the benefits of NFV deployment to your telecom enterprise. We'll help you seamlessly build, deliver, update, and support your NFV software.

The platform empowers service providers with improved agility, simplicity, and elasticity in how new revenue-generating services are brought to market—all while reducing costs.
